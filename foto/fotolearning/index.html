Photography through four decades by Matti Kärki

Could you have captured my photos?

My work embraces minimalism and abstract expression, often infused with subtle humor or fleeting spontaneity.

Each photograph evokes a specific emotion in me. I have grouped them by theme to offer a thoughtful journey—moving beyond mainstream styles to reveal glimpses of the world as I see it.

I primarily shoot with a Canon 5D Mark IV and EF L-series lenses, occasionally using old Nikon gear. Editing is done in Lightroom Classic, with no use of generative AI.

Based in Vantaa, Finland.
