Minimal Lens

Photography through four decades by Matti Kärki

Could you have captured my photos?

Perhaps. But we all see the world a little differently.

My work leans toward minimalism and abstraction, often shaped by light, form, and small moments of spontaneity — sometimes even a touch of humor.

Each photograph carries a feeling for me. I have grouped them by emotion, not by genre, to offer a different kind of journey.

I don't use any generative AI.

Based in Vantaa, Finland.
