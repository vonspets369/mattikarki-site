Hermetic Minimalism

Photography through four decades by Matti Kärki

My work embraces minimalism and abstract expression through a hermetic lens, often infused with subtle humor or fleeting spontaneity. I search for the ancient signal behind the noise. My photography is an attempt to tune my kame-RA back to a primordial frequency—one that opens the mind with the light that comes from our star.

Each photograph evokes a specific emotion in me. I have grouped them by theme to offer a thoughtful journey—moving beyond mainstream styles to reveal glimpses of the invisible layers of the world as I see and feel them.

I don't use any generative AI.

Based in Vantaa, Finland.
