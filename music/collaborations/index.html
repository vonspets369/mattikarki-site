COLLABORATIONS

In the early 1990s, I acquired basic music production and recording skills at a studio in Haparanda, Sweden, while attending Finska Folkhögskolan. Since then, music has been a constant passion in my life, and I've been actively involved in various musical projects almost every year. I've collaborated with numerous musicians over the years and here are only some projects where I left my fingerprint as a technician, composer, musician or producer.

I feel my best comes out when I'm with other creative people and the energy goes from person to person. My brain and fingers usually work fast in executing ideas, and I trust my gut feeling when trying different things in music production. It's always about finding something cool!
